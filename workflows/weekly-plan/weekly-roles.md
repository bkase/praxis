# Weekly Roles (v2) — Weekly Nourishment Lens (not a rigid schedule generator)

Use these as a **weekly balance checklist** after floors + project anchors are planned.
Pick 0–2 roles that feel neglected and add small optional menu items (not a full schedule).

- **Partner**: time and dates with Gigi
- **Health & Energy**: sleep, diet, exercise, recovery
- **Builder**: craft, creating, coding, shipping artifacts
- **Student**: learning, study, fundamentals (e.g., Karpathy block)
- **Friend**: friends, calls, hangouts
- **Content Creator**: build/learn in public; writing, demos, posts
- **Explorer**: discomfort reps; new places/ideas (bounded)
- **Personal Growth**: reflection, planning, journaling
- **Admin**: life admin, finances, maintenance
- **Family Member**: broader family
- **Community Member**: coworking, meetups, like-minded spaces
- **Missionary**: work tied to your broader purpose/arc
